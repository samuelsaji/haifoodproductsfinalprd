Characteristics of our charcoal-dried meat

Our charcoal-dried meat is made entirely at home with the collective cooperation of family members, following traditional methods. No chemicals are added to preserve taste, color, or prevent spoilage, ensuring a unique and natural flavor. The meat is sliced thinner than market alternatives, allowing it to dry better and retain a superior texture. All waste (unwanted parts) are carefully removed, leaving only the finest cuts to dry, ensuring a high-quality, delicious product.

Characteristics of our shrimp Chammanthi powder

Our Shrimp Chammanthi Powder is made with fresh, washed, and dried shrimp, prepared by family members in a traditional, rustic manner. No chemicals or preservatives are added for taste, integrity, or color, ensuring an authentic and natural product. Delivered directly to customers or via courier as per the order, it is not available in shops, preserving its freshness and homemade quality.
